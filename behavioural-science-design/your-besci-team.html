Meet your BeSci team

British Gas has been collaborating with Cowry Consulting for more than two years to integrate Behavioural Science into every customer touchpoint, while also leveraging it to enhance the overall customer experience.

We have a strategic programme of upskilling, leading on journey workshops, practical application and participation in Cowry-led Behavioural Science forums and book clubs, which all form the building blocks of continuous development of internal Behavioural Science capability.

Our Behavioural Science Bootcamp

Celebrating Behavioural Science application and capability building at British Gas Energy

Bootcamp is all about celebrating how far we’ve come with behavioural science and embedding it across British Gas Energy. To share our journey with Cowry and what we’ve achieved in under three years.

We have our masterclass practitioners volunteer to talk about how they’ve implemented behavioural science, share their successes and spark a few ideas of what we could do more of or differently.
